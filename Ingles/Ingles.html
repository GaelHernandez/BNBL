About

The BNB Lottery project is the NFT lottery in which users can win up to 80 times the minting value of their participating NFT.

The winning Wallet will be the one that has the numerical NFT of the BNB Lottery of the current edition, which coincides with the decimals of the last close of the BNB/USD pair on the day following the Sold Out of the current edition.

BNB LOTTERY

In each edition a total of 100 NFT will be sold and the winning Wallet will take 5 BNB.

Each BNB Lottery numerical NFT has a minting value of 0.06 BNB and each Wallet can hold as many as they want.
